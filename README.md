# 📊 Tata GenAI Powered Data Analytics Job Simulation – Forage

This repository documents my completion of the GenAI Powered Data Analytics Job Simulation with Tata iQ (Financial Services Team) on Forage.
The project showcases how AI‑driven analytics can be applied to risk profiling, predictive modeling, and collections strategy design in financial services.

---

## 🚀 Overview

- Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using GenAI tools to assess data quality, anomalies, and delinquency risk indicators.
- Proposed a no‑code predictive modeling framework for delinquency risk assessment.
- Designed a business report and storytelling strategy for collections, tailored for stakeholders.
- Outlined a high‑level AI‑driven collections system with ethical guardrails and measurable business impact.

---

## Task 1: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Risk Profiling

- Reviewed dataset structure and identified missing/inconsistent data.
- Detected anomalies (e.g., unusual credit utilization, incomplete payment history).
- Highlighted early risk indicators:
   - High credit utilization
   - Missed/late payments
   - Low income‑to‑debt ratio
- Proposed imputation/synthetic data generation strategies to handle missing values.

## Task 2: Predictive Modeling Framework

- Outlined a logistic regression model (baseline, interpretable) with option to extend to random forest (complex, higher accuracy).
- Selected top 5 features: payment history, credit utilization, income level, account age, credit inquiries.
- Justified logistic regression for transparency, compliance, and stakeholder trust.
- Defined evaluation metrics: Accuracy, F1 score, AUC, fairness checks with bias mitigation strategies.

## Task 3: Business Report & Data Storytelling

- Top 3 Risk Factors:
   - High credit utilization
   - Irregular payment history
   - Low income‑to‑debt ratio
- SMART Recommendation: Reduce delinquency among high‑utilization customers by 10% within 12 months via proactive monitoring and targeted repayment reminders.
- Ethical AI Considerations:
   - Bias against low‑income groups → fairness audits
   - Over‑penalizing temporary hardships → human review + hardship programs
- Ensured plain‑language explanations for non‑technical stakeholders.

## Task 4: AI‑Driven Collections Strategy (Executive Deck)

- System Workflow: Customer data → Risk scoring → Automated actions → Learning loop.
- Agentic AI Roles:
   - Autonomous: reminders, risk scoring, repayment suggestions
   - Human Oversight: hardship reviews, escalations, compliance checks
- Responsible AI Guardrails: fairness audits, explainability, regulatory compliance, human‑in‑the‑loop.
- Expected Impact:
   - KPIs: 10–15% delinquency reduction, cost savings, improved efficiency
   - Customer Outcomes: fair treatment, personalized support, enhanced trust
